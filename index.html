Tonight We Ride Presents

Chad D's 26th Annual Toy Drive

Y'all ready to rock? If so, please join us for a night of great music for a great cause. 7 bands and the show is free with an unwrapped toy or a monetary donation of ANY size at the door. If you are strapped for cash, come anyway because you will not be turned away.

The Bands

  • RTB2
  • Rhett Miller (Vid from NYC)
  • Ottoman Turks
  • Nothing Strikes Back
  • Armchair Rebels
  • Meat Sweats
  • New Ellum

Details

This is year 26, and the proceeds will benefit Dallas Life homeless shelter and Family Place domestic abuse treatment center. Every year is epic for not only the music, but also the showing of love and compassion during the holiday season. Come rub elbows with some of the best musicians in Dallas and show up hungry because the Barley House food is amazing. The pizzas and the wing sandwich are legendary. First band is at 6:30 sharp for the early birds and last band at 11:30 for the all-nighters. Come one, come all, because this year is gonna be huge.
